Less Frequently Asked Questions

February 2026 · Part of the LLM Guide for Humanities Scholars

Last updated: February 2026

The FAQ addresses the questions colleagues raise most often. This page tackles questions that are less common but no less important—questions that probe deeper into what these systems are, how they work, and what they mean for scholarly practice.

Each question was put to several leading AI models, using the same wording, and responses are presented side by side. This allows you to compare not only what the models say but how they say it—what they emphasise, what they omit, and where they agree or diverge. The differences are often as instructive as the answers.

About these responses: Each model was given the same question with no additional context or system prompt beyond the platform defaults. Responses were collected on the dates shown and have not been edited. Models may give different answers if asked again; these are snapshots, not definitive statements.
